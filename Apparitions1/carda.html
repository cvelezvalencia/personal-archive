YOU HAVE FOUND OUR LADY OF SAN JUAN

She will watch over when you are under the knife.

In 1899 an interior malady surprised Desideria Vasquez who, having no medicine left to relieve it, was forced to resort to surgery. Before her operation, she recommended herself to our Lady of San Juan. Because our Lady intervened favorably, she has dedicated this plaque to her. San Luis 1901.
