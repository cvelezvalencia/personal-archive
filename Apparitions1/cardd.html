YOU HAVE FOUND OUR LADY OF SAN JUAN

She will will ward off thieves.

After they left mass the 7th of June of 1942, sisters Rosa and Carmen Ruiz were crossing the alley behind the market when they were assaulted by a man with a gun, to avoid getting killed they immediately summoned the Lady of San Juan de los Lagos. The man only took their purses, which had nothing in them except a few coins and their prayer books, and did not hurt them at all; they thank the virgin for her good work with this plaque.
